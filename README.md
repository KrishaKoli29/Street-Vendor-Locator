These days it is extremely easy to search for restaurants, cafés or even ghost kitchens. But what about mobile shops and stalls that do not have a permanent location. Street food, which lies at the heart of every Indian person, is a complete chance-based industry. Using our project, we wish to fill this gap by providing a live, GPS-based tracking system for the Street vendors. 

Previous attempts at such a technology have been limited to particular localities and due to lack of proper marketing and funds have not been able to remain functioning.

Also, we noticed that many vendors and hawkers lack the understanding of technology to keep up with their accounts and update regularly. We change that by making the interface and functioning as simple as possible.

With the rise in prices of food at fancy indoor restaurants, Street Food is like a haven to those who cannot afford such luxuries. It is important to keep the culture of street selling as it is a core part of livelihood as equally for the customers as it is for the vendors.
